Robin Williams

The man who made an entire generation smile

About Robin's life

  • Williams is often considered one of the greatest comedians of all time.
  • Williams began his career as a stand up-comedian in the 70s, and rose to national attention after his role on the ABC sitcom, Mork and Mindy.
  • After his breakout performance as the lead actor in Popeye, Williams became one of the world's most sought-after actors, performing classic films such as Good Morning, Vietnam, Dead Poet's Society, Aladdin, Mrs. Doubtfire, Jumanji, and Good Will Hunting.
  • Outside of the movies, Williams was still an avid performer, from his Broadway appearance, music career, and Audible talk show.
  • Outside of his career, Williams was as equally impactful. He was supporter of women's rights, veteran's rights, and literacy programs. Moreover, he was a regular supporter of the St Jude's Children Hospital and helped organize donations to aid New Zealands affected by the 2010 Canterbury Earthquake.
